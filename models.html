models of bmw

Z 4

Captivating aesthetics accompanied by overwhelming self-confidence characterize the BMW Z4 First Edition. This view is crowned by the hexagonal Bi-LED headlights with vertically arranged tubes and for the first time flexibly aligned with the side, thus opening the view of the central area: the BMW grille Flat-lower kidney with mesh design. While the M-style air vents add more width to the front end, they give the car an exclusive sporty flavor designed specifically for the roads.

5 Series

The BMW 5 Series Sedan takes sportiness and self-confidence to the next level thanks to its modular proportions such as the long wheelbase, extended bonnet and streamlined roofline. Fine lines add subtle contrast to flat, low surfaces, deepening the impression of a purely modern look. In particular, the kidney-shaped front grille and BMW laser lights give the front of the car its dynamic, innovative character.

X models of bmw

X 1

The BMW X1 impresses with its everyday urban features and compact exterior dimensions that hide much more than you can possibly imagine. . Another element that underscores this feeling is the high visibility due to the height of the seats and the large window areas throughout the car. However, the impression of the BMW X1 is not purely objective: 56 centimeters of legroom in the rear means comfortable seating on long journeys, even for tall passengers.


X 2

The BMW X2 creates its own style and sticks to it along the way: its streamlined shape and modern dynamism are embodied in its unique sporting character, all in combination with innovative design and impressive proportions. It's perfect for anyone who wants to discover new trends that no one else has tested yet. Undoubtedly innovative, exceptional in the extreme. The BMW X2 will captivate you with its soul Sports at first sight.

X 3

The BMW X3 is itself an epitome of unlimited opportunity, an expression of the ultimate in presence and absolute freedom. At first sight, its sporty appearance impresses you and captivates you with the exciting driving experience it offers you. Thanks to the partially autonomous driving technologies and the intelligent all-wheel drive BMW xDrive system, the BMW X3 offers absolute comfort both on and off-road. It thus redefines the standards well known in its category and proves one unambiguous truth: the difference between one method and another to reach your destination is vast.

X 4

The exterior design unequivocally confirms the BMW X4's passion for excitement.The front air intakes give the car a sense of grandeur and control thanks to the car's ideal size and three-dimensional design. They stand out clearly from the bonnet when driving, giving the car a uniquely dynamic character. The exterior air vents also add a distinctly sporty look. The wheel pod has been designed in a different way from the previous square design to give the BMW X4 the off-road adventurous spirit. The air outlets behind the front wheels and the large 21" light alloy wheels Double-spoke 718M in Cerium Gray Duo high-gloss will definitely catch your eye.

It is the BMW X5 concept car in which every detail is a statement of perfection. Electric Models

iX xDRIVE50

With a charging capacity of up to 200 kW (BMW iX xDrive40: up to 150 kW), you can recharge the battery of your BMW iX at public fast-charging stations from 10 to 80% of total capacity in less than 40 minutes. At home, you can recharge your BMW iX xDrive50 using your convenient BMW Wallbox with up to 11 kW – and reach 100% battery charge in under 11 hours (BMW iX xDrive40: in under 8 hours)

BMW i3

Featuring an all-electric range roughly between 182 and 188 miles*, eDrive technology, innovative LifeDrive architecture and the use of organic materials, the BMW i3 is impressive from every angle. Plus with 170hp, which goes from zero to 62mph in 7.2 seconds, and no traditional driving gears, the special feeling that comes with driving electric can be felt straight away.

iX3 Premier

With an all-electric range of up to 282 miles* and an enhanced eDrive unit, the first-ever BMW iX3 Premier Edition is ready to explore. With 286hp and going from zero to 62mph in 6.8 seconds, this new all-electric driving experience also comes with BMW X model genes, such as extra room for longer journeys.

BMW i4

With up to 530 hp, the BMW i4 delivers scintillating performance, going from zero to 62mph in just four seconds. With a range of up to 366 miles* (WLTP), the first-ever pure electric five-seater Gran Coupé offers generous headroom, extra legroom and enough space for up to three people in the rear. It really is perfect for both longer journeys and everyday use.
